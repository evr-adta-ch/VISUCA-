The Ahmadou Ahidjo's Stadium

Editor’s pick

The Ahmadou Ahidjo stadium is an architectural gem located in the heart of Yaoundé, the capital of Cameroon. This iconic stadium, named after the country's first president, is a place steeped in history and passion for football.

As soon as you enter the stadium, you are immediately struck by its majesty. The imposing stands rise around the playing area, providing stunning views of the pitch from every angle. With a capacity of more than 40,000 seats, the Ahmadou Ahidjo stadium is the ideal venue for hosting major national and international sporting events.

The impeccably maintained lawn is worthy of the largest football fields in the world. Players can enjoy perfectly flat and green terrain, offering optimal playing conditions. The stands are designed so that every spectator can enjoy excellent visibility, creating an electric atmosphere during the matches.

Apart from football matches, the Ahmadou Ahidjo stadium is also used for various cultural and musical events. Spectacular concerts take place here, attracting thousands of enthusiastic fans. The stadium's modern facilities, such as high-quality sound equipment and giant screens, make it a versatile venue for all kinds of events.

Finally, the history of the Ahmadou Ahidjo stadium is closely linked to that of the Cameroonian nation. Since its inauguration in 1972, it has witnessed many unforgettable moments of joy and triumph for the national football team. Passionate fans fill the stands during matches, creating an incredibly festive and encouraging atmosphere.

In short, the Ahmadou Ahidjo stadium in Yaoundé is much more than just a sports venue. It is a symbol of national pride, a place where dreams come true and memories are made. From thrilling football matches to memorable cultural events, this stadium offers an unforgettable experience to everyone who visits it.

The Olembe Stadium

Editor’s pick

Get ready for a spectacular sporting experience in a breathtaking setting. Welcome to Stade d'Olembe, the architectural jewel of Yaoundé that combines cutting-edge technology, modern aesthetics and sporting passion. Let me take you on a journey at the heart of this marvel where beauty and innovation meet.

As soon as you arrive, you will be dazzled by the imposing structure of Olembe Stadium, whose bold design rises proudly into the sky. Its elegant curves and flowing lines make it a true work of modern art. But this stadium is not only an architectural marvel, it is also equipped with the most advanced technologies to provide an unparalleled experience.

Once inside, you will be transported to a world of emotions and excitement. The immense stadium enclosure envelops you in its vibrant energy, while the stands offer breathtaking panoramic views of the pitch. You will be immersed in an electric atmosphere, whether during fiery football matches, captivating concerts or memorable cultural events.

The beauty of Olembe Stadium also lies in its harmonious integration with the surrounding nature. Nestled in the middle of vast green spaces, it offers breathtaking views of the lush landscapes of Yaoundé. You can stroll around the surroundings, recharge your batteries in the peaceful gardens and connect with nature before or after the events.

But this stadium is not limited to its exterior appearance. Inside, you will be amazed by the world-class facilities that combine comfort, technology and security. Ergonomic seats provide optimal comfort for spectators, while state-of-the-art giant screens allow you to fully immerse yourself in the action. State-of-the-art sound systems ensure exceptional audio quality, amplifying the intensity of highlights.

Olembe Stadium is also a model of sustainable innovation. Eco-friendly technologies are used to reduce the stadium's carbon footprint and promote sustainable development. Solar panels are installed to provide renewable energy, water recovery systems are put in place for responsible use of resources and green spaces are developed to promote harmony with the environment.

Whether you are a sports enthusiast, a concert goer or simply looking for an unforgettable experience, the Stade d'Olembe in Yaoundé is the ideal place for you. Come vibrate to the rhythm of the action, let yourself be inspired by the architectural beauty and experience magical moments that you will never forget.
