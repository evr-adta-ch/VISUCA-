Imagine yourself strolling along winding paths, surrounded by lush vegetation. The majestic trees rise to the sky, their branches dancing harmoniously with the light breeze. The sun's rays slip through the foliage, creating a magical play of light that illuminates your path.

As you explore this green sanctuary, your senses will be amazed by a natural symphony. The song of exotic birds echoes through the trees, creating a haunting melody that transports your mind to faraway lands. The intoxicating scents of tropical flowers and plants tickle your nostrils, inviting you to breathe deeply and allow yourself to be soaked in their splendor.

For lovers of outdoor activities, Bois Sainte Anastasie offers a multitude of possibilities. Whether you want to take a revitalizing hike, have a picnic with the family or simply relax reading a book under the friendly shade of the trees, this magical place is your haven of peace.

Bois Sainte Anastasie is much more than a simple green space, it is an invitation to contemplation and wonder. It’s a place where you can escape the hustle and bustle of everyday life and reconnect with nature in all its splendor. Whether you are a nature lover, an adventurer or simply looking for serenity, Bois Sainte Anastasie is a treasure to discover.

Don't wait any longer, come explore this natural wonder in the heart of Yaoundé and let yourself be enchanted by the timeless beauty of Bois Sainte Anastasie.

The Plants's Garden

Editor’s pick

Prepare to immerse yourself in an enchanting world where nature reveals itself in all its splendor. Welcome to the Jardin des Plantes de Yaoundé, a true botanical treasure that transports you on an extraordinary sensory journey. Let me guide you through this paradise plant where beauty and innovation meet.

From the moment you arrive, you will be captivated by an explosion of colors and captivating aromas. The carefully landscaped gardens invite you to explore an endless variety of exotic and native plants, each revealing its own history and beauty. From vibrantly hued roses to delicate orchids to majestic palms, each species seems to compete in grace and brilliance to catch your eye.

But the Jardin des Plantes goes well beyond a simple collection of plants. It is a place of innovation where the preservation of biodiversity and the protection of the environment are at the heart of concerns. Research and conservation projects are carried out here, helping to save many valuable plant species. You can discover the latest scientific advances in the field of botany and raise awareness of the importance of preserving our ecosystem.

As you stroll through the shaded paths of the garden, you will be amazed by the diversity of the landscapes that unfold before you. From crystal clear waterfalls to peaceful ponds, flower-lined paths to themed gardens, every corner of the garden is an invitation to escape. You can lose yourself in the magic of a serene Japanese garden, relax in the tropical ambience of a lush greenhouse, or simply sit on a bench and soak up the tranquility here.

The Jardin des Plantes is also a place of discovery and learning. Guided tours, workshops and interactive exhibitions are offered to allow you to deepen your knowledge of the world of plants. You can learn the secrets of pollination, understand the life cycles of different species, and even participate in hands-on activities such as planting and tending gardens.

Whether you are a nature lover, a botanical enthusiast or simply looking for a peaceful escape in the middle of the city, the Jardin des Plantes de Yaoundé is a true gem. Come get lost in its enchanting paths, let yourself be inspired by the diversity of the flora and discover the eternal beauty of nature.

The Mont FEBE park

Editor’s pick

Prepare yourself for a true natural getaway that will transport you to heights of beauty and serenity. Welcome to Parc du Mont Fébé in Yaoundé, a true natural treasure that offers you a unique experience in the heart of lush nature. Let me guide you through this haven of peace where beauty and innovation meet.

As soon as you arrive, you will be captivated by the majesty of Mont Fébé which stands proudly before you. Its steep peaks blend harmoniously with the azure sky, forming a breathtaking landscape. As you embark on the park's trails, you will be enveloped by the lush greenery and fresh air, providing you with a true natural refuge in the heart of the city.

The Parc du Mont Fébé is much more than just a green space. It is a place of exploration and discovery where you can lose yourself in the diversity of ecosystems found there. From dense forests to sunny clearings, from refreshing waterfalls to peaceful lakes, every corner of the park has surprises and moments of contemplation in store.

As you stroll through the winding paths, you can discover exceptional flora and fauna. From exotic birds to colorful butterflies, wildflowers to ancient trees, every encounter with nature connects you with the very essence of beauty. You can indulge in photography, capturing magical moments and creating unforgettable memories.

But the Parc du Mont Fébé is not limited to its enchanting landscape. It is also a place of innovation where technology blends harmoniously with nature. Modern infrastructures are integrated in a responsible manner, respecting the ecological balance. Solar panels provide clean energy, waste management systems are put in place to preserve the environment, and ecological trails are created to minimize the impact on flora and fauna.

The Parc du Mont Fébé is also a place of rejuvenation and well-being. You can practice yoga on top of a hill, meditate near a soothing waterfall, or simply relax on a lush green lawn while soaking up the tranquility here. It’s a great place to escape the hustle and bustle of everyday life and rediscover a deep connection with nature.

Whether you are a nature lover, a hiking enthusiast or simply looking for a peaceful place to recharge your batteries, the Mont Fébé Park in Yaoundé is a true oasis. Come and lose yourself in its natural beauty, let yourself be inspired by the serenity that reigns and discover the regenerative power of nature.
